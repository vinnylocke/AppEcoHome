## Description  *(max 4000)*
```
Rhozly is the calm, all-in-one home for your garden — part plant encyclopaedia, part task planner, part AI plant doctor. Whether you tend three houseplants or a whole veg plot, Rhozly tells you exactly what to do today and helps every plant thrive.

KNOW WHAT TO DO, EVERY DAY
• A single dashboard shows today’s tasks, your local weather, and every area of your garden at a glance.
• Smart, weather-aware reminders for watering, feeding, pruning and more — so nothing gets forgotten.
• A guided “Garden Walk” takes you plant by plant, so caring for everything takes minutes.

AI PLANT LENS
• Snap a photo and Rhozly identifies the plant, spots what’s wrong, and suggests the care it needs.
• Diagnose pests, disease and deficiencies in seconds — then turn the fix into a task.

GARDEN AI — AN ASSISTANT THAT ACTS
• Chat with an assistant powered by Google’s Gemini Pro models — our most advanced model yet is exclusive to Evergreen — and it knows YOUR garden.
• It checks your plants, tasks, weather and soil sensors, answers anything, and sets up schedules, plans and automations for you to confirm with one tap.
• Every answer follows the same clear layout: the answer first, what it checked, what’s ready to confirm, and one suggested next step.

EVERY PLANT, ORGANISED
• Catalogue your whole collection with photos, notes and journals.
• Track seedlings in the Nursery from sowing to planting out.
• Keep an Ailment Watchlist of the pests, diseases and invasives to watch for.

PLAN & DESIGN YOUR SPACE
• Draw your garden to scale in a 2D & 3D layout editor, with sun and microclimate insight per bed.
• Build seasonal plans, get companion-planting suggestions, and a Sunday “week ahead” overview.
• Measure real light levels with the Light Sensor and follow the sun with the AR Sun Tracker.

WEATHER THAT WORKS FOR YOU
• Local forecasts with frost and heatwave alerts, tuned to your plants and your hemisphere.

GROWS WITH YOU
• Expert step-by-step guides for every skill level.
• Connect smart-garden sensors and valves to see soil moisture and automate watering.
• Share a garden with family or housemates, each with their own permissions.

Start free with Sprout. Upgrade to Botanist, Sage or Evergreen for AI features, deeper planning and higher limits.

Rhozly works beautifully on phone and tablet, online or off. Plant smarter. Grow more. Enjoy your garden.
```
