Everything your garden needs, in one app

Today's tasks, your weather and every area of the garden — at a glance.

Gemini-Pro-powered chat that knows your garden — it checks your data, answers anything, and stages changes for one-tap confirmation.

Snap a photo to identify a plant and diagnose pests, disease or deficiencies.

Plant collection

Catalogue every plant you grow with photos, notes and journals.

Smart reminders

Weather-aware watering, feeding and pruning tasks so nothing's forgotten.

Garden Walk

A guided round of the garden — care for everything, plant by plant, in minutes.

Ailment Watchlist

Track the pests, diseases and invasives to watch for before they spread.

Draw your plot to scale, with sun and microclimate insight for every bed.

Live soil-moisture sensors and automated watering valves.

Harvest & yield

Harvest windows, yield logging and season totals for every crop you grow.
