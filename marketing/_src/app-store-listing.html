Description ≤ 4000
Rhozly is the calm, all-in-one home for your garden — part plant encyclopaedia, part task planner, part AI plant doctor. Whether you tend three houseplants or a whole veg plot, Rhozly tells you exactly what to do today and helps every plant thrive. KNOW WHAT TO DO, EVERY DAY — one dashboard shows today's tasks, your local weather and every area of your garden; smart, weather-aware reminders for watering, feeding and pruning; a guided "Garden Walk" takes you plant by plant. AI PLANT LENS — snap a photo and Rhozly identifies the plant, spots what's wrong and suggests care; diagnose pests, disease and deficiencies in seconds, then turn the fix into a task. GARDEN AI — AN ASSISTANT THAT ACTS — chat powered by Google's Gemini Pro models (our most advanced model yet is exclusive to Evergreen) that knows YOUR garden: it checks your plants, tasks, weather and soil sensors, answers anything, and sets up schedules, plans and automations for you to confirm with one tap — every answer in the same clear layout. EVERY PLANT, ORGANISED — catalogue your collection with photos and journals; track seedlings in the Nursery; keep an Ailment Watchlist of pests, diseases and invasives. PLAN & DESIGN — draw your garden to scale in a 2D & 3D layout editor with sun & microclimate per bed; seasonal plans, companion-planting tips and a Sunday "week ahead"; measure light with the Light Sensor and the AR Sun Tracker. WEATHER THAT WORKS FOR YOU — local forecasts with frost and heatwave alerts, tuned to your plants and hemisphere. GROWS WITH YOU — expert guides, smart-sensor & valve integrations, and shared gardens with per-member permissions. Start free with Sprout. Upgrade to Botanist, Sage or Evergreen for AI features, deeper planning and higher limits. Works on phone and tablet, online or off.
