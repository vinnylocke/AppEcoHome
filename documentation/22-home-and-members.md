# Homes & Members

A **home** in Rhozly is a garden — its locations, plants, tasks, weather and everything else all belong to one home. Most people have a single home, but you can keep several (e.g. a house garden and an allotment) and share any of them with the people you garden with.

**Home Management** is where you switch homes, manage the people in each one, and edit a home's details.

Access it from the **account menu → Manage homes** (route `/home-management`). It has three tabs: **Settings**, **Insights**, and **Members**.

> ℹ️ Note: this is different from the **Location Manager** (`/management`), which is about the *places and areas inside one home*. Home Management is about the homes themselves and who can see them.

---

## Multiple homes

- Every account starts with one home, created during onboarding.
- Create another with **+ Add Home** — give it a name and location; Rhozly sets up its own weather, hemisphere and empty garden.
- Your **active home** is the one everything else in the app is showing. Switch it from Home Management, or from the **home switcher** in the header.
- Homes are fully separate — plants, tasks and members in one home never leak into another.

---

## Inviting members

Open a home's **Members** tab and choose **Invite member**. You can:

- **Send an email invite** — they get a link to accept and join.
- **Share a join link** — copy it and send it however you like (message, email). Anyone with the link can join with the role you set, until you revoke it.

New members appear in the Members list once they accept.

---

## Member roles

Each member has a **role** that sets what they can do. From least to most access:

| Role | Typically can… |
|------|----------------|
| **Viewer** | See the garden — plants, tasks, weather — but not change anything. Good for someone you just want to keep in the loop. |
| **Member** | Everything a Viewer can, plus complete tasks and log day-to-day activity (photos, notes, harvests). |
| **Admin** | Manage the garden — add and edit plants, tasks and schedules, and manage other members. |
| **Owner** | Full control of the home, including its settings and members. Every home has an Owner. |

Rhozly also supports **per-member permissions** — fine-grained toggles (for example "view members' personal tasks" or "view all members' activity") that layer on top of the role. Set a member's role, and adjust any individual permissions, from the **⋯ / configure** control next to them in the Members list. The exact capabilities each role grants are shown there at the point you assign them.

---

## Changing a role or removing a member

- **Change a role** — pick a new role from the member's row in the Members list. It takes effect immediately.
- **Remove a member** — an Owner (or a member with the right permission) can remove someone from the Members list; their access is revoked at once, and their data in *other* homes is untouched.
- **Leave a home** — remove *yourself* with **Leave home**. If you're the Owner, transfer ownership or remove the other members first.

---

## Common questions

**Does inviting someone cost anything or use their tier?** No — members work within *your* home. Their own subscription tier only affects homes they own.

**Can two people edit at the same time?** Yes. Changes sync in near-real-time, so a partner completing a task on their phone shows up on yours.

**What happens to shared data if a member leaves?** Anything they added (plants, photos, notes, completed tasks) stays in the home — only their access is removed.
